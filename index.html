FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Optimus bot is an algo trading bot that can integrate with any Binance account and trade in it. Once the bot is integrated with your Binance account, it will trade automatically 24/7 without any human intervention. The USP of Optimus bot is it's unique trading strategy and execution prowess which has resulted in 36% ROI annually for the last 3 years. User can stop the bot anytime by disabling the API keys in their Binance account. The trading funds stay safu in user's Binance wallet. Optimus bot doesn't ask for permissions to withdraw funds from your Binance.

To integrate the bot with a Binance account, user needs to create trading API keys in Binance account and enter them in the Optimus bot app. While creating API keys in Binance, user has to allow trading permissions for the bot, but withdrawal permission is not allowed. Upon successful integration of bot with Binance account, the bot starts trading based on it's algorithm. Users can view realtime trade positions in their Binance account or Optimus app. Optimus App displays profits in the intervals of Daily and Monthly.

Your trading fund is always safe in your Binance account. You never need to send trading fund to the bot. The bot will trade in your Binance account through trading API keys that you provide during the bot setup. The bot never asks withdrawal permission in the trading API keys.

User needs to buy subscription to access the bot. The subscription charge is very nominal like $50 for portfolio value upto 5000 USDT, $100 for portfolio value between 5000 to 10000 USDT. This subscription charge amount needs to be deposited in the Optimus App as USDT (BEP20).

Optimus bot works on profit sharing model. The bot takes 40% profit share from all the trading profits. User gets 60% profit share. Suppose a user has trading portfolio of 1000 USDT. If in one month the total profit generated by the bot is 100 USDT, then 60 USDT is the user's profits share and 40 USDT is the bot's profit share.

As Optimus bot works on profit sharing model taking 40% of the total profit generated. But the trading funds and profit funds stay in user's Binance account only. The bot cannot withdraw the funds in user's Binance account. Therefore there is a concept of Recharge in the Optimus App for the bot to deduct it's profit share. User needs to recharge some USDT upfront in the Optimus App, most common recharge amount is 100 USDT. Now whenever the bot makes profit on trades in the user's Binance account, bot deducts value owrth 40% of the profit amount through this recharge USDT. Gradually the recharge money finishes and user needs to topup again to continue using the bot.

Our claim is based on the past performace of the bot. No one can guarantee the profit percentage in trading, even the biggest mutual funds or portolio managers cannot assure a fixed profit. Optimus bot has been active since 4 years and users have got profits since last 3 years consistently. Based on the past performance of the bot, it generates on average 3% monthly profit and 36% annual profit. All the trades are visibile to the user in realtime in their Binance or Optimus App. Users can see their trading postions, floating profit and loss positions in realtime.

Ensuring the funds safety to the users has been our top priority. The trading strategy of our bot follows low risk approach. As the users have to keep their funds in Binance, so the safety of your Binance account is crucial. And Binance being a centralized exchange, there is an inherent risk of a central point of failure. If Binance as an exchange continuous operation as normal, there can be no issue in the functioning of Optimus bot. 36% ROI is the feature of Bot 1.0 model of Optimus App. In Bot 1.0 model, there are only 4 coin pairs being traded viz. BNB/USDT, DOGE/USDT, MATIC/USDT and XRP/USDT. These coins have the most credible reputation and history in the crypto market.
